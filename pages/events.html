Extreme Calendar

Where Legends Are Made

Extreme sports events are more than competitions — they are moments where courage, precision and raw passion collide. From the frozen peaks of world-class snowboarding venues to the open skies conquered by skydivers and wingsuit pilots, each event pushes human limits further.

Our global calendar brings together the most prestigious championships and iconic locations on the planet. These are the stages where champions rise, records are broken and legacies are forged against gravity, speed and snow.

Whether it’s freefall at terminal velocity, silent flight through mountain gaps or gravity-defying tricks above icy halfpipes — this is where extreme becomes eternal.

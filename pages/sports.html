Wingsuit

Become the Bird

Wingsuit flying is the art of transforming vertical speed into horizontal distance. Using a specialized jumpsuit that increases your body's surface area, you can glide through the air with incredible efficiency.

It requires not just courage, but a deep understanding of aerodynamics and precise body control.

Glide Ratio

Modern wingsuits allow for glide ratios of 3:1 or better, meaning for every foot you drop, you can travel three feet forward. This unlocks the potential for proximity flying and long-distance travel.
